Panchenko Ilya Sergeevich




About me


Panchenko Ilya was born in Sicily in 1887.
Ilya grew up with his best friend Frank Vinci and they almost always did everything together.
When both turned 20, they decided to move to America, where Panchenko began to train and take part in fights for money,
Vinci also began to settle in the "circle" of the mafia. A couple of years later, Panchenko became a professional fighter and champion.
But Vinci did not waste time either - by 1931 he had founded his family, and Ilya offered the position of his consigliere, and Panchenko agreed.
But after a couple of months, Vinci decided to make good money on bootlegging and ordered Leo to take up this business.
In 1943, Panchenko was arrested for "purchased" fights and sentenced to five years in prison.
In prison, he became a respected person, right after the head of the prison, with whom Ilya got along well.
There, Ilya organized training fights and, based on his experience, began to teach other “convicts” new tricks.
It was there that Panchenko met his new assistant, Pepe, who was his best fighter among the rest.
